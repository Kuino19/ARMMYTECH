The Problems We Solve

Why Healthcare Operations Are Broken

etiQ targets four critical inefficiencies baked into traditional healthcare administration and patient workflows.

Compounding Wait Times

Bottlenecks during triage, check-in, and patient routing pile up — leaving patients waiting for hours, creating a frustrating and stressful hospital experience.

Clinical Administrative Burnout

Medical staff spend significant time managing physical queues, organising patient files, and handling manual documentation instead of focusing purely on patient care.

Predictive Inefficiency

Standard hospital queues are purely reactive. They struggle to adapt dynamically when emergencies arise, appointments run over, or departments face sudden patient volume surges.

Lack of Queue Transparency

Patients are frequently left in the dark about their actual wait times, which lowers satisfaction and dramatically increases perceived friction in the care journey.

What We'll Achieve

Transforming Hospital Operations

etiQ aims to turn healthcare facilities into seamless, data-driven environments by hitting four measurable milestones.

40%

Reduction in Wait Times

By optimising how patients are sorted, prioritised, and routed through departments using intelligent queueing algorithms — not guesswork.

AI-Powered Clinical Assistance

Automating administrative micro-tasks for healthcare providers, allowing doctors and nurses to capture insights and move through consultations more fluidly.

Dynamic Resource Allocation

A predictive dashboard that balances patient loads across available staff and rooms in real time — minimising idle time and preventing over-saturation.

In Progress — Detomi Hospital

Validated Pilot Success

Proving these metrics in real-world clinical environments to establish a repeatable, scalable model for healthcare facilities globally — starting with Detomi Hospital.
