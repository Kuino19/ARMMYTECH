Kompli is an AI-powered legal compliance platform designed to make regulatory compliance simple, affordable, and accessible for African businesses. With Kompli, businesses can navigate complex legal requirements without the need for expensive legal teams.

Legal. Simple. Smart.

  • AI-assisted compliance guidance tailored to African regulations

  • Affordable pricing designed for SMEs and startups

Why Compliance Matters

Legal compliance is one of the biggest barriers for African businesses — Kompli removes it.
