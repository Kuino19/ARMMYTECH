About A&I Tech

Innovating from Africa, for Africa — and the world.

How It Started

Built from a Shared Vision

Armstrong & Ifedayo Tech LTD was founded with a clear purpose: to build technology that genuinely improves how African businesses operate. Our co-founders — Lawal John Ifedayo and Alfred Armstrong — came together from complementary backgrounds: deep technical expertise and strategic business leadership.

They saw a gap — African businesses struggling with compliance, inefficiency, and lack of accessible technology tools. Rather than wait for solutions to come from outside, they decided to build them from within.

Today, A&I Tech is building a portfolio of software products designed for African realities — affordable, reliable, and smart.

Our Founding Principle

Africa doesn't need to wait for technology from elsewhere. The solutions to Africa's challenges will be built by Africans — with world-class standards and local understanding.
